Unfiltered

Hi, I'm Megan — I post way too much about makeup + skincare, test way too many lip tints, and somehow turned my K-beauty obsession into content people actually watch (still wild). I make videos and posts about makeup, skincare, and everything in between — from honest reviews to “is this viral product actually worth your money?” kind of chaos.

I'm not here to gatekeep anything. If it makes my skin look glowy or my makeup routine faster, I'm sharing it. Expect soft glam looks, budget-friendly faves, some unhinged product rankings, and the occasional rant about sunscreen texture.

My Origin Story

I didn't plan on becoming the friend everyone texts at 2 a.m. asking “is this toner actually worth it?” — but here we are. My dive into skincare started somewhere between trying to cure high school breakouts and watching one too many 10-step K-beauty routines on YouTube. Spoiler alert: I did not need 3 essences, but the obsession stuck.

What started as “just trying to get rid of a pimple before picture day” spiraled into a full-blown love for ingredients, textures, and packaging that looks like it belongs in a candy store. Eventually, I started sharing my trial-and-error chaos online — and somehow people actually cared?! Now I make content that's honest, a little unhinged, and super obsessed with anything that promises glow.
